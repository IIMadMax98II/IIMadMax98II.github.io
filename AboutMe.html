About Me

Hello, my name is Mariano Cayzac. I am a game developer with great passion for game design. I have developed a great variety of small games and projects to further develop and boost my abilities on all branches of game development.

As a graduate bachelor in computing, I have adquired good analyitcal skills, speed for learning and gathered solid knowledge on both C# and C++ programming languages, different frameworks, game engines and methodologies.

My Greatest Asset

What I believe to be my greatest asset is that I always try to find ways to better myself. Looking out for things I can learn or improve, becoming more efficient, independent and reliable.

What I have gathered from my education

It was through my education that I became a better team player, participating in multiple game projects and game jams. Furthermore, I developed the ability to swiftly identify, assess and troubleshoot any obstacle that may arise during production and conveying the information reliably and precisely to my teamates.

My biggest weakness

My ability to always come up with new and original ideas often gets me to loose scope and come up with ideas that are too big, however my studies have taught me the skills needed to identify scope overstimations and rapidly narrow down to a minimum viable product. ; Thus starting small,identifying most important concepts and leaving space for future additions if needed and time allows it.
